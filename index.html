I'm an Electronics and Communication Engineer specializing in C/C++ firmware and hardware-software integration. With hands-on professional experience analyzing commercial fire alarm control networks, I bridge the gap between physical hardware constraints and scalable system logic.

My focus is on architecting fault-tolerant, multithreaded IIoT edge nodes using FreeRTOS, building thread-safe data pipelines, and deploying asynchronous cloud telemetry — all while staying obsessed with performance and reliability at the silicon level.

I thrive at the intersection of embedded systems and full-stack development. My passion lies in engineering robust, end-to-end solutions that are as technically sophisticated as they are practical for the user.

// Featured Project 01

IIoT Safety Controller & Edge Node

Architected a fault-tolerant ESP32 safety controller utilizing FreeRTOS preemptive scheduling to decouple asynchronous MQTT cloud telemetry from time-critical hardware interrupts. Engineered thread-safe data pipelines using RTOS Queues and Mutexes to ensure deterministic sensor sampling while maintaining zero packet-loss telemetry upload via WiFi. Deployed on Wokwi for hardware simulation.

FreeRTOS ESP32 MQTT C++ IIoT PlatformIO
